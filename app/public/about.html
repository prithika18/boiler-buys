About Boiler Buys

Welcome!

Welcome to Boiler Buys! On our website you can buy and sell all you university needs. We will break down each button below.

User Table

Fill out the new user table on the Boiler Buys Homepage to create an account with BB using your first and last name in addition to your email address.

Sell/Buy Table

If you click on the Sell/Buy Portal you can upload a product and sell it! The table below allows you to search from the availible products by category. It will list the current availible items for purchase in the table below.

Review Product Table

By clicking on the Review Products button you can review the products you have purchased by providing a description and rating.

Tutor Table

By clicking on the Tutor Sign-Up button you can become a tutor on Boiler Buys by inputting your information and skillset.

Roommate Table

By clicking on the Roommate Portal button you can put in your expect graduation date, interests, noise level, guest amount to help you find the right roommate.

Product Request Table

By clicking on the Product Request button you can put requests for products thay you are interested in and are willing to buy on Boiler Buys. Someone may have that product and can sell it to you.
